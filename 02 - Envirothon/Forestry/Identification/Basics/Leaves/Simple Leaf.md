![[simpleleaf.jpg]]

Above is a *Simple Leaf*. The long thin line marked A is the leaf stalk (also called [[Petiole]]). It's the part where the leaf goes out on its own from the stem. The circular bit marked B is called the [[Axillary Bud]]. It can indicate whether something is just a collection of leaves (simple leaf), or just one leaf with many leaflets (compound leaf).
