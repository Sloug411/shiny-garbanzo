There are several types of leaf margins. Entire leaf margins have no teeth (sharpy bits) nor lobes (roundy bits). Dantate have sharp teeth that point directly out. Serrate have sharp teeth pointing forward (remember serrated haha). Double Serrate have it where each sharp forward tooth has smaller teeth. Lobed leaves have large rounded projections sorta like ear lobes (double haha!).
