**Beginning**
At the beginning of the story Naruto is a young, eager kid, itching for power in Konoha. When his [[Genin Exams]] roll along he is tricked by [[Mizuki]], an assistant teacher of his, to steal a sacred scroll. While waiting to hand Mizuki the scroll, Naruto learns his most iconic technique, [[Shadow Clone Jutsu]].His teacher, [[Iruka]], finds Naruto, tells him Mizuki lied, and informs him of his carrier status. Through Naruto's bravery when facing Mizuki he earns the status of [[Genin]].

**Early Genin Days**
A day after graduating from the [[Academy]], Naruto learns the identity of his teammates, [[Sasuke Uchiha]], [[Sakura Haruno]], and his [[Jonin]] sensei [[Kakashi Hatake]]. After a team-building exercise, the group, now having the moniker of [[Team Seven]], start to preform [[E-Rank Missions]].

**Land of Waves**
The [[Sarutobi Hiruzen|Hokage]] calls Team Seven to his office after performing a sufficient amount of missions. He calls for them to escort a man named [[Tazuna]] to the [[Land of Waves]] as a [[D-Rank Missions|D-Rank Mission]]. On the road to Tazuna's village the group are assaulted by a pair of [[Missing Nin]]. Kakashi pretends to die, but the team defeat the enemies anyways. After interrogating Tazuna he finally reveals his true motives, his town wants to build a bridge, but they are too poor to pay for an [[A-Rank Missions|A-Rank Mission]] for protection against thugs. Team Seven decide to proceed with the mission, even with the increase in difficulty. The group then are attacked by [[Zabuza Momochi]], an incredibly strong Missing Nin. He is defeated by Kakashi, but right before his death he is saved by a [[Land of Mist|Mist]] [[Bounty Hunter]]. The "woman" informs the group that she has been tracking down Zabuza for a time, and will take him into custody. Kakashi subsequently passes out, exhausted from the battle. Team Seven then take residence in Tazuna's house for the rest of the mission. When Kakashi awakens he reveals that Zabuza is still alive, as the Bounty Hunter was a fake. While protecting the bridge, Kakashi teaches Team Seven the [[Tree-Climbing Technique]], a manipulation of [[Chakra]] that allows the user to walk on walls. While alone Naruto meets a "woman" picking flowers named [[Haku]]. After spending the day with them, Haku reveals that he is truly a man. A few days later, Kakashi, Sasuke, and Sakura are attacked by Zabuza and the fake Mist Bounty Hunter. Naruto overslept that day and was sleeping at Tazuna's house. After learning about the attack Naruto rushes to the scene, finding Kakashi engaged in combat with Zabuza, Sasuke fighting the false Bounty Hunter, and Sakura protecting Tazuna. Naruto join's Sasuke's fight and gets trapped in [[Crystal Ice Mirrors]], the secret [[Jutsu]] of the Bounty Hunter. Sasuke uncharacteristically dives in the way of a volley of [[Senbon]] to save Naruto's life, gaining his [[Sharingan]] in the process. Naruto becomes enraged, generating some of the [[Kurama|Kyuubi's chakra]] as a power-up. Naruto punches the Bounty Hunter, shattering their mask and revealing Haku. Before Naruto can hurt Haku any further, Haku jumps infront of a [[Chidori]] meant for Zabuza. The Missing Nin then realizes the errors of his ways as a cause of someone he considered a son dying for him. Zabuza then murders [[Gato]], a corrupt man that hired him to stop the building of the bridge.

**Chunin Exams: First Exam**
After returning home Team Seven learn of an opportunity to advance their militaristic rank, the [[Chunin Exams]]. After entering the exam building, the Team meet their fellow examinees, the rest of the [[Konoha Eleven]] and Ninjas from a variety of villages. Naruto has a short interaction with man named [[Kabuto Yakushi]], a [[Village Hidden in the Sound|Sound]] Ninja. The first test is an exam proctored by [[Morino Ibiki]], a leader in the [[Konoha Torture and Intelligence|T&I Department]]. The exam is outrageously difficult, but the true test is to see how well the examinees can cheat. Naruto does not notice this and completes the exam normally. At the end, Ibiki asks whether anyone would like to drop out of the exam and many do. Naruto jumps on his desk and persuade the remaining people to never give up. He passes the first exam even though he did not get a single question right, as the only requirement was to not give up.

**Chunin Exams: Second Exam**
The second exam is a challenge to traverse the [[Forest of Death]] with the goal of getting to a tower in the middle of the forest with two types of scrolls. After about a day of wandering around in the forest, Naruto decides to pee. While he pees, an [[S-Rank Shinobi]], [[Orochimaru]] impersonates him. The team easily figure out the man's true identity and begin to fight him. Meanwhile Naruto is attacked by a large snake. Orochimaru sheds his skin and leaves a large bite on Sasuke's neck, a [[Seal]] named the [[Cursed Seal of Heaven]]. Sasuke becomes increasingly angsty, but the group advance to the next round.

**Chunin Exams: Third Exam**
The third exam is a tournament between the remaining Genin. Naruto wins in his match against [[Kiba Inuzuka]] by farting on him. The Genin then take about a month break before recontinuing the tournament. In the mean time Naruto meets [[Jiraya]], a [[Sanin|Legendary Sanin]] that becomes his teacher. Jiraya teaches Naruto the [[Summoning Jutsu]] and [[Water Walking Technique]]. In the final match between a [[Village Hidden in the Sand|Sand]] [[Shinobi]], [[Gaara]], and Sasuke it is revealed that Orochimaru murdered the [[Kazekage]], impersonated him, and planned an invasion on the [[Village Hidden in the Leaves|Leaf]]. Orochimaru forces Gaara to unleash his [[Tailed Beast]], the [[Shukaku|Ichibi]] on the village, destroying it. The Sound Village are also controlled by Orochimaru, with him being their [[Kage]], and thus start also invading the leaf. Naruto runs after Gaara summoning a giant [[Toad]] named [[Gamabunta]] to fight the [[Jinchuriki]]. After defeating it, Naruto convinces Gaara to be a good person.
