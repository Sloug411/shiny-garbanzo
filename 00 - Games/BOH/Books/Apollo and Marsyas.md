#Heart 

![[apollomarsyasbook.png]]
**Description**: "The libretto of a lost opera concerning the contest between [[Apollo]] and [[Marsyas]], and its tragic outcome. The librettist is identified only by their initials: RKNJL."

**Study**: "In the original myth Marsyas lost a musical contest and got flayed by Apollo, who later regretted it. In this version Marsyas' skin has a further history..."

**Mastery**: "The skin of Marsyas gives oracles, and is later smuggled to [[Phrygia]], where the priestesses of Cybel use it as a drum head. At the opera's climax, the pounding of the drum drives would-be violators to suicidal madness. The opera ends with a wistful hymn from the youngest priestess on the beauty of mountain pines."

**Analysis**: The most interesting thing about this book is what it reveals on [[Heart]]. Typically, Heart is a lively, healing [[Principles|Principle]], so it is very curious that an object that has the aspect drives people to "suicidal madness." At first, I suspected that the drum is of dual-Principle, Heart and [[Winter]], due to the death that is caused by the instrument, but after thinking more about the two Principles, I rethought this possibility. Heart and Winter are inherently antithetical to one another, one being a Principle of music, dancing, and eternity, while the other is of silence, the dead, and endings. If an object contained both of these principles, I would think the dualistic nature of it would be more prominent. Apollo and Marsyas inspire many questions on what Heart is.
