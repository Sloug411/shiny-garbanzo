Was Marsyas already involved in the occult before his flaying, or was he oblivious to it all? The answer can be most likely found in the fact that he was once presumably a musician, a career heavily entwined with [[Heart]]. Why does the beating of the drum bearing Marsyas' skin provoke suicidal tendencies? Heart is most commonly a principle of protection and everlasting life, so it is quite out of character for the principle to make someone want to end themselves, a theme much more alike [[Winter]].
