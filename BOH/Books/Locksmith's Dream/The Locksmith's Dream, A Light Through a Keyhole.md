#Lantern #Unfinished 

![[locksmith1.png]]
**Description**: The first volume of Teresa Galmer's examination of parallels in the dreams of artisans.

**Study**: "The parallels in mystic dreams experienced by carpenters, masons and other artisans, and what they purport to reveal about the architecture of the world. Sometimes mordant, sometimes funny. No-one has ever explained why Galmier devoted herself to this quixotic exploration of artisan's dreams."

**Mastery**: "Time and again we hear of the [[Wood]], which rises from the world's foundation. All trees reach for light. What does the Wood reach for? Is there a difference between light and [[The Glory|Light]]? I think the key to dreams of the Wood might be one of these - the one that isn't exactly real."
