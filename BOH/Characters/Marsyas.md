Little is revealed about Marsyas' early life, but it can be concluded that he is Greek, due to his connection with Apollo. Although his life is not heavily documented, his death and the whereabouts of his body are. According to [[Apollo and Marsyas]], he once had a musical competition with Apollo, and got flayed because of it. His skin was then aquired by a group of priestesses in Phrygia, and used as the skin of a drum. Considering that Marsyas was flayed, one can confidently say that after his death he is [[Heart]] aligned, but when he was alive is a bit more blurry. If he ever followed a [[Principles|Principle]] during his life, it would most likely be Heart, due to the fact that he was a musician.
