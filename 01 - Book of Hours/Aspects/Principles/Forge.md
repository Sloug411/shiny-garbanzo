#Forge #Unpolished 

![[forge.png]]

Forge is the [[Principles|Principle]] of rebirth and sacrifice. Forge rituals often describe destroying something to create stronger things. This can be seen most prominently with the story of [[The Lionsmith]], where a great warrior destroys his sword to become a [[Hours|Hour]]. The most important Forge Hour is [[The Forge of Days]], a [[Gods from Light|God from Light]] whom the Principle was named after.
