#Lantern #Unpolished 

![[lantern.png]]

Lantern is the [[Principles|Principle]] of knowledge and learning. Followers of Lantern are known to have an insatiable hunger for more knowledge on the arcane. There are three [[Hours]] which are tied for the most important Lantern Hour, [[The Door in the Eye]], [[The Egg Unhatching]], and [[The Sun-in-Splendour]]. Each is the most prominent Lantern Hour in its own era. The Door in the Eye is the top Lantern Hour in the modern era, The Egg Unhatching was the top Lantern Hour in the pre-[[Lithomachy]] era, and The Sun-in-Splendour was the top Lantern Hour in the pre-[[Intercalate]] era.
