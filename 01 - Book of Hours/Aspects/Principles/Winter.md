#Winter #Unpolished

![[winter.png]]

Winter is the [[Principles|Principle]] of stillness and death. Winter adherents tend to be incredibly morose and serious. The most prominent Winter [[Hours|Hour]] is [[The Elgiast]]. [[Solomon Husher]] describes in his book [[On The White]] that Winter has a "long doomed romance" with the "Sun." Solomon can mean three things by this, Winter has a doomed romance with either the [[Lantern]] aspect or the [[Glory]].
