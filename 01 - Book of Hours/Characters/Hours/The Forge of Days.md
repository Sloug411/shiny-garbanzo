#Forge #Unpolished

![[forgeofdays.png]]

The Forge of Days is one of the [[Gods from Light]] that descended from the [[Glory]] in the [[Lithomachy]]. Her principle is [[Forge]], and it is heavily suspected that the principle was named after her. Before the [[Intercalate]], she was [[The Sun-in-Splendour]]'s lover. She is described in the [[The Burning of The Unburnt God]] as a "pre-Zoroastrian fire deity," possibly signaling a history in the Middle East.
